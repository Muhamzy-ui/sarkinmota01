Logo Extraction Tool

This tool perfectly isolates the gold 'M' and text from your original photo, removing the ceiling background smoothly to create a 100% transparent PNG.

Instructions:
  1. Click the button above and select the original photo you provided.
  2. The script will process the pixels instantly to remove the background.
  3. Click the green Download button.
  4. Move the downloaded `logo-gold.png` into this actual project's `public` folder, replacing the current one.

Note: Since this runs directly in your browser, nothing is uploaded to the internet and no downloads were required.
